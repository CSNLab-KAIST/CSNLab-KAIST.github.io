Welcome to the CSNLab!

Welcome to CSNLab, where we focus on three key research areas: next-generation Computer Architecture, with emphasis on deep learning accelerators and memory systems; high-performance Interconnection Networks, particularly Network-on-Chip designs for heterogeneous systems; and Hardware Security, investigating micro-architectural vulnerabilities in GPU and NUMA architectures. Join us in advancing computing systems!
